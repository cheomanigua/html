Background

Know a bit about my overall past experiences that led me to my present.

I've been surrounded by computers since a very early age. My first lines of code were issued in a MSX computer in a big store when I was 12 years old. From that moment, I knew computers were going to be a part of my life. I owned a MSX, a Commodore 64, a Commodore Amiga 500 and then I jumped to MSDOS PCs. I've used all kinds of OS: Windows, MacOS, Linux, Solaris, FreeBSD and OpenBSD. Right now my main OS is Ubuntu.

My experience with computers comes from all trades: building PCs, installing all kind of OS, gaming, making mods for games, scripting languages, modeling and texturing. I wanted to show off my work, so I learnt to build websites with HTML, CSS and Javascript. I tinkered with several game engines: Unreal Engine, Unity and Godot.

After studying PHP and web development on college, I got interesed on SaaS. I ended up working on G Suite solutions and interested in all things related to Cloud. I was sold off on Docker and Kubernetes.
