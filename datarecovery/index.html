From our online form, or through a phone call or directly at our store, you can fill out your request. Details of the problem and the affected unit will be recorded. Free Diagnosis
With the first valuation carried out and with the unit in our hands, we will make a complete diagnosis to assess the extent of the damage and the costs of data recovery.
